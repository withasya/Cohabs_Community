Our purpose

We care about our members and that’s what drives us to do better every day.

At Cohabs, we believe in bringing people together and creating meaningful connections. Our international community is composed of expats as well as locals coming from all over the world. Today, we've had over 3000 people stay with us and become a part of our thriving community. We're always looking to improve the Cohabs experience, and our members play a huge part.

The 6 universes of our community

You will never be bored. Worst case scenario, you will dream.

LEARN


We believe that being curious and learning from each other are key to true inclusivity and respect.

ACT


We don't do big speeches. Instead, we take action for causes that we hold dear.

COOK


Food has been the best way to gather people and create meaningful connections since the dawn of time. So don't underestimate the power of cooking together!

DREAM


Dream big, no one has ever achieved greatness without dreaming.

SHARE


True change starts with you. Share your dreams, share the love, and share what you're passionate about.

PARTY


Life is a party. At Cohabs, we celebrate every chance we get!

Everything you need at your fingertips

The Cohabs app allows you to open your door, request maintenance, and check rent payment. It’s also how you connect with your housemates!
  • Lock and unlock your door
  • Chat with your housemates
  • Report an incident
  • Manage all your lease details
  • Switch rooms or houses at any time
  • Join or create local and global events and groups
